About E-Summit 2025

An annual entrepreneurship summit bringing together aspiring entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and innovators

Why E-Summit?

E-Summit 2025 is a movement to foster entrepreneurial culture and innovation. Organized by E-Cell SIT, this summit bridges the gap between aspiring entrepreneurs and the startup ecosystem.

From hands-on workshops to thrilling competitions, we offer experiences that inspire, educate, and empower.
