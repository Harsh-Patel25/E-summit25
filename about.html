About E-Summit 2025

Igniting the Entrepreneurial Spirit

Why E-Summit?

E-Summit 2025 is more than just an event—it's a movement to foster entrepreneurial culture and innovation among students. Organized by the Entrepreneurship Cell of Saffrony Institute of Technology, this summit aims to bridge the gap between aspiring entrepreneurs and the startup ecosystem.

Whether you're a budding entrepreneur, a startup enthusiast, or simply curious about the world of innovation, E-Summit offers something for everyone. From hands-on workshops to thrilling competitions, we've designed an experience that will inspire, educate, and empower you.
